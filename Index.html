Jawhar Navodaya Vidhyalaya


Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas are a system of central schools for talented students predominantly from rural area in India. They are run by Navodaya Vidyalaya Samiti, New Delhi, an autonomous organization under the Department of School Education and Literacy, Ministry of Education, Government of India.
